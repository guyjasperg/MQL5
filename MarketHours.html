Market Closure Notes

Weekends: The Forex market is generally closed from 22:00 UTC (Friday) to 22:00 UTC (Sunday) for retail traders.

Holidays: Major national holidays (e.g., Christmas, New Year, Independence Days) can cause low liquidity or full market closures in specific sessions.

TIP: The first few hours of the Sydney session on Sunday can have wider spreads due to low liquidity as banks re-open.
